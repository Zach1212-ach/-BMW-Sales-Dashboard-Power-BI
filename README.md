# 🚗 BMW Sales Dashboard – Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
A Power BI dashboard analyses BMW sales performance by area, model, and market trends. Over the course of three report pages, the research discusses macroeconomic market insights, unit sales trends, and revenue KPIs.
---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview
![Executive Overview](executive_overview.PNG)

- 💰 **Total Revenue** — overall sales revenue KPI card
- 📦 **Total Units Sold** — volume of cars sold
- 📊 **Avg Price per Car** — average selling price
- 📈 **YoY Revenue Growth %** — year-over-year comparison
- Revenue Trend by Year (line chart)
- Sales by Region (donut chart)
- Slicers for interactive filtering

---

### 2. Sales Analysis
![Sales Analysis](sales_analysis.PNG)

- **Revenue by Model** — ranked bar chart across BMW models
- **Units Sold by Month and Region** — clustered column chart
- **Revenue Matrix: Region vs Model** — pivot table for cross-analysis
- Slicers for dynamic filtering by region/period

---

### 3. Market Insights
![Market Insights](market_insights.PNG)

- **Electric Vehicle Adoption Trend** — line chart showing EV growth
- **Revenue vs GDP Growth** — scatter chart (macro correlation)
- **Fuel Price Impact on Sales Volume** — combo line & column chart
- **Premium Segment Share by Region** — bar chart

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Power BI Desktop | Data modeling & visualization |
| DAX | KPI measures (Revenue, YoY Growth, Avg Price) |
| Excel / CSV | Source data |

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- YoY Revenue Growth tracked to measure business momentum
- Regional breakdown reveals strongest and weakest sales markets
- EV adoption trend signals shift in BMW's product mix
- Fuel price correlation provides context for volume fluctuations
- Premium segment share highlights positioning in luxury market
